Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of sliced bread
  • 2 slices of american cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of softened butter

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium-low, while youre waiting, generously spread softened butter on one side of each bread slice
  2. Once pan is hot enough, add bread slices in buttered-side down
  3. carefully place cheese slices on one of the pieces of bread, then place other bread on top of it
  4. close lid on pan and wait for about 3 minutes, flip the sandwhich with a spatula
