Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sundried tomatoes
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • approx. 1 lb of chicken breast or boneless thigh
  • cajun seasoning
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/3 of an onion

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan on medium heat, add oil add in chicken breast and sear until golden brown
  2. Take out chicken breast and place in seperate container, now add in onions and garlic to pan
  3. Once onionsa nd garlic look translucent, also add in sundried tomatoes and continue to saute for 5 minutes
  4. Add in chicken broth and reduce until spatula can scrape bottom of the pan
  5. Turn heat to low and add in cream
  6. Add chicken back in and continue to heat on low for 5 minutes
