Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • cooking oil
  • approx 1/4 cup milk or water

Steps

  1. First, heat up pan on medium. While you wait, crack eggs and milk into bowl and whisk whisk whisk!
  2. Once pan is heated up and mixture consistency is relatively uniform, add mixture into the pan and start scraping away
  3. Stir, stir stir, and mix, mix, mix until the eggs are firm!
  4. Pour out and serve!
